How it works

Webcam to voice
in five steps.

Every step runs locally, end-to-end, in under 4 milliseconds per frame.

01📷

Capture

Webcam streams live at 30fps.

02✋

Detect

MediaPipe maps 21 hand points.

03🧠

Predict

The model reads the points, returns a letter.

04💬

Build

Letters form words on screen.

05🔊

Speak

Web Speech reads it aloud.
